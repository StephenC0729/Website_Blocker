1. Information We Collect

Bolt Blocker collects the following information to provide our website blocking service:

Personally Identifiable Information

  • Google Account Data: Email address, name, and profile information when you sign in with Google OAuth
  • User ID: Unique identifier for your account and data synchronization

Web History

  • Visited URLs: Websites you visit to determine if blocking should be applied
  • Blocked Sites List: Custom list of websites you choose to block
  • Website Access Attempts: Records of when blocked sites are accessed

User Activity

  • Blocking Events: When and how often sites are blocked
  • Timer Sessions: Focus session durations and break patterns
  • Usage Statistics: Productivity metrics such as time saved and blocking effectiveness
  • Extension Interactions: How you use the extension features and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Block websites according to your preferences
  • Sync your blocked sites across devices
  • Provide usage statistics and productivity insights
  • Send notifications about blocked sites
  • Maintain your account and preferences

3. Permission Usage

App Blocker requires the following browser permissions:

  • Identity: Sign in with Google for secure authentication
  • Tabs: Monitor which websites you visit to enforce blocking
  • Active Tab: Check the current tab's URL against your block list
  • Notifications: Alert you when sites are blocked or time limits are reached
  • Storage: Save your blocked sites list and extension settings locally
  • Firebase Endpoints: Sync your data securely across devices via Google Firebase

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally in your browser and synced to Google Firebase for cross-device access. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information. Your browsing data never leaves your device except for the specific URLs you choose to block.

5. Third-Party Services

Bolt Blocker integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For secure data storage and real-time synchronization across devices
  • Google OAuth: For secure user authentication and account management
  • EmailJS: For sending productivity reports and notifications via email

These services are subject to their respective privacy policies. We do not share your personal information with any other third parties beyond what's necessary for these integrations to function.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and blocked sites list until you delete your account. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the extension settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your blocked sites list
  • Opt out of data collection by uninstalling the extension

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the extension's support page.
